The Problem
Our memories are far from perfect. A year from now, will you remember the web campaigns that made you successful? Will you remember how your competitors were trounced or how they left you wondering why you failed? Will you remember the designs you tested and how your site evolved? You must know the past to plan the future.
The Solution
Recite provides software as a service to allow an enterprise to archive public web pages it is interested in tracking over time. Recite allows you to search for and review these archived versions to see how they've evolved. Recite is like the Internet Archive’s Wayback Machine but, it is under your control. You decide what to collect, when to collect it and who can access your archive.
Features
Collect
Recite gives you the power to choose the web pages it archives. Each archive is called a Recital. The Recital Edit screen allows you to describe the page URLs you want to archive, the frequency of archival, and the page width you want capture. You can also sign up to receive email notifications each time the page is archived.
Compare
Recite provides easy ways to search and browse through your archive. Archived pages can be compared side by side to see what has changed on a page. You can also compare how your site stacked up to the competition in the past.
Analyze
Recite delivers compelling analysis tools. The evolution of often subtle visual changes are easy to detect. For instance, you can overlay two pages to see differences and you can highlight changes with the click of a button.
Archive
Recite presents an overview of your archive in a Dashboard. Each Recital is shown in a tile with the latest captured page's thumbnail displayed. The Dashboard provides quick links to create or edit Recitals and to search or browse your archive.
